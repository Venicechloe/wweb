FACILITIES

OUR FACILITIES

Our school provides state-of-the-art facilities to ensure that students have the best environment to learn, explore, and grow. Explore our classrooms, laboratories, and recreational spaces below.

Library

Our well-stocked library provides students with access to a wide range of books, journals, and digital resources to support learning and research.

Dressmaking Laboratory

A creative space where students learn sewing, clothing design, and basic fashion skills for hands-on training.

Computer Laboratory

The computer lab is equipped with the latest technology to help students develop digital skills for academics and future careers.

Gymnasium

Our gym provides students with a safe space to engage in physical activities, sports, and wellness programs.
